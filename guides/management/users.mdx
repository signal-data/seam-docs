## Inviting a new team member
To invite a new team member, click the **Invite people** button.

## Authentication
Seam prioritizes security by using Auth0, the industry standard for identity management. We support the following login methods:

1. **Username & Password:** Log in using a standard email address and password.
2. **Google Workspace:** Log in using the Google account associated with your organization.
