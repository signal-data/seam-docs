Complete these steps to start generating pipeline with Seam AI. Most teams finish onboarding in 1-2 weeks.

## Step 1: Connect your data sources

Connect your CRM and any sales or marketing tools you use. Seam requires at least one CRM to get started.

**Required:** Salesforce or HubSpot  
**Recommended:** Sales engagement tools, marketing automation, data warehouse
