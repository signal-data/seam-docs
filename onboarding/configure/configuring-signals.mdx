## Create a Signal

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Signal Type">
    Choose which signals are relevant to your sales cycle.

    **Audience and Persona-based**
    - Job change
    - New hire
    - Job opening
    - Promotion

    **Keyword-based**
    - Social listening
    - Funding round
    - Social engagement
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Signal-Based Plays">
    Build automated workflows that execute when signals fire
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Monitor and Refine">
    Track which signals lead to pipeline and optimize accordingly
  </Step>
</Steps>
